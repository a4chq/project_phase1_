Description about the above vehicles

    Electric Vehicle (EV)
  • A vehicle that runs on electric power, using batteries to store energy.
    • Hybrid Vehicle
  • A vehicle that combines an internal combustion engine with an electric motor.
    • Luxury Car
  • A high-end car known for its premium features, comfort, and performance.
    • Sports Car
  • A small, lightweight car designed for high performance and speed.
    • SUV
  • A sport utility vehicle designed for both on-road and off-road capabilities.
